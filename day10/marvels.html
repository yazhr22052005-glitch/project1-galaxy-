Marvels


wolverine

Wolverine He is a mutant with animal-keen senses, enhanced physical capabilities, a powerful regenerative ability known as a healing factor, significantly delayed aging and prolonged lifespan. In addition to the X-Men, Wolverine has been depicted as a member of X-Force, Alpha Flight, the Fantastic Four and the Avengers.


Deadpool

Deadpool is a popular Marvel Comics character known for his humor, sarcasm, and unpredictable behavior. His real name is Wade Wilson.He’s famous for his red and black suit, twin katanas, and love for chaos. Deadpool is a complex antihero who struggles with pain, love, and doing the right thing in his own twisted way.


Venom

Venom is one of Marvel’s most iconic antiheroes, known for his dark and powerful appearance. He’s an alien symbiote that needs a human host to survive. Though often seen as a villain to Spider-Man . His deep voice, sharp teeth, and monstrous form make him one of the most unforgettable characters in the Marvel universe.


DareDevil

Daredevil is a Marvel superhero known as the “Man Without Fear.” His real name is Matt Murdock.By day, he fights for justice in the courtroom, and by night, he protects his city as Daredevil. Daredevil stands as a symbol of courage and resilience despite his blindness.
