ecommerce management system

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, is the process of buying and selling goods or services using the internet. It encompasses online transactions facilitated by websites, mobile apps, and marketplaces like Amazon and Flipkart, offering a convenient and accessible way to conduct business and shop globally. E-commerce includes various models, such as business-to-consumer (B2C), business-to-business (B2B), and consumer-to-consumer (C2C) transactions, and is a fundamental part of the modern digital economy.
